## Calibration & Coverage | Q1–Q2 2027

- [ ] **Continuous calibration loop**
  - [ ] <span className="pink-glow-text">Compare</span> QQ Scores against realized performance, by bucket and time horizon  
  - [ ] Tune weights, thresholds, and methodology with strict versioning and rollback  
  - [ ] Track stability across regimes, including bull and bear markets and high and low volatility environments

- [ ] **Data reliability and coverage**
  - [ ] Expand coverage only with <span className="pink-glow-text">quality</span> gates, including freshness, confidence, and anomaly filters  
  - [ ] Show metric confidence so strategies can require a minimum quality level


## Personal QQ OS | Q3–Q4 2027 

- [ ] **Portfolio and watchlist overlay**
  - [ ] User inputs holdings and watchlists  
  - [ ] QQ shows an aggregated <span className="pink-glow-text">risk profile</span> and key red flags  
  - [ ] Turn user objectives into portfolio rules and a <span className="pink-glow-text">allocation and rebalance plan</span>  
  - [ ] Enforce guardrails, including risk budgets, exposure limits, and liquidity and slippage constraints

- [ ] **Explainability**
  - [ ] Automatic <span className="pink-glow-text">“why it happened”</span> analysis, including PnL attribution and an event trail  
  - [ ] Decision trace showing which QQ signals triggered which actions
